## Logging convention

- Hours rounded to 0.5h.
- "ai-arch" hours = career/cert/POC/promo/interview prep (i.e. everything *other than* GE delivery work + sleep + family + fitness).
- GE delivery hours not tracked here in detail (assumed default ~8h/day, see roadmap goal #2). Logged only when materially over (e.g. P0 firefight days).
- Fitness logged in **sessions** (not just hours) — quality > duration.
- "Δ" = actual − planned. Negative = behind plan.

## Velocity rules of thumb

- **Behind 1 week** = make up next week. Don't auto-extend deadline.
- **Behind 2 consecutive weeks on cert** = re-check Mock #1 viability; if ≤4 weeks to mock with <50% study done → push exam to Jun 21.
- **Behind 2 consecutive weeks on health (lbs)** = revisit calorie/sleep adherence in [check-ins.md](check-ins.md); not just hours.
- **Eugene artifact >2 weeks idle** = breaks the gatekeeper signal; force a session.
- **POC slip beyond May 24** = de-scope nb_05; ship nb_01–nb_04 only.
