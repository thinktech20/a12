## Phase 2 interview ammo — what each "1-day stage" produces

Time-boxed comparisons designed to generate concrete interview talk tracks. NOT exploration for its own sake.

| Stage (1 day each) | Output | Interview question it answers |
|---|---|---|
| **Parsing alts** — PyMuPDF vs pdfplumber vs Unstructured on the same FSR page | Side-by-side output diff + 1-page write-up | "How do you handle messy PDFs?" / "What parser would you pick and why?" |
| **Embedding model swap** — text-embedding-3-large vs text-embedding-3-small (or another) on same chunks | Score deltas, dim trade-off, cost comparison | "How would you decide between embedding models?" / "What about model upgrades?" |
| **Chunking variants** — extend nb_02b with structure-aware vs recursive vs semantic chunkers | Findings table + when-to-use-which | "How did you pick chunk size?" / "What chunking strategy and why?" |

Each stage = ~4h work + 30 min write-up. Together they generate 3 strong concrete answers vs hand-wavy "I considered alternatives."
