# Health & Fitness — May → Sept 2026

Goal: **−20 lbs by Sept 30** (stretch: Aug 31). Plus better sleep, more energy, less afternoon crash, mental health. Coupled to the work plan in [roadmap.md](roadmap.md) — fitness directly feeds cognitive performance, which feeds cert + interview prep.

Starting baseline: mostly sedentary, gym-membership-but-not-going, generally healthy, no flags. **Baseline weigh-in: Sun May 10 morning** (fasted, post-bathroom, log in [check-ins.md](check-ins.md)).

## Phased approach (do NOT skip ahead)

### Phase 1 — Habit + sleep (May 12 → Jun 30, ~7 weeks)

**Goal:** show up consistently, fix sleep, don't get hurt. **No weight target this phase.**

**Workouts — 4×/week, 45 min each, gym**

| Day | Session | What |
|---|---|---|
| Mon | Strength A | Goblet squat · DB bench press · 1-arm DB row · plank · 10 min walk warmup |
| Tue | Cardio | 30 min steady-state (treadmill incline walk / bike / rower) at zone 2 (can talk in full sentences) + 10 min stretch |
| Thu | Strength B | DB Romanian deadlift · DB overhead press · lat pulldown · side plank · 10 min walk warmup |
| Fri | Cardio + mobility | 25 min cardio + 20 min mobility/stretching |
| Sat or Sun | Outdoor | 60 min walk/hike — kid can join from May 15 |

**Reps/sets for strength:** 3 sets × 8–12 reps. Stop 2 reps short of failure (RPE 7–8). Add weight when 12 reps feels easy.

**Sleep — non-negotiable**
- Lights out 22:30, up 06:00 = 7.5h
- Phone OUT of bedroom (charge in another room)
- No screens 21:30 onward
- Caffeine cutoff **14:00** sharp — single biggest sleep lever

**Diet — minimal rules (no tracking yet)**
- Protein at every meal — palm-sized portion (chicken / fish / eggs / paneer / dal+rice combo / Greek yogurt)
- 2L water/day
- No alcohol on gym nights (Mon/Tue/Thu/Fri)
- Caffeine cutoff 14:00
- Eat slowly, 15-min minimum per meal
- Optional lightweight calorie estimate in `check-ins.md` (intake / exercise burn / net) from `madhu-log`; keep it simple, no strict dieting targets in Phase 1.

**Phase 1 success = showing up 4×/week + sleeping 7.5h.** Scale doesn't matter. Energy + mood + sleep quality are the metrics.

**Expected by Jun 30:** −3 to −6 lbs, dramatically better energy, sleep solid, no injuries.

### Phase 2 — Strength + composition (Jul 1 → Aug 31, ~9 weeks)

**Goal:** add load, eat with intent, lose visible fat. Target **−10 to −15 lbs total by Aug 31**.

**Workouts — 5×/week now (3 strength + 2 cardio), 45–60 min**

Switch to a real beginner barbell program (5×5 / Starting Strength style) once form is solid:

| Day | Session |
|---|---|
| Mon | Strength A: squat 5×5 · bench 5×5 · row 5×5 |
| Tue | Cardio: 35 min zone 2 OR 20 min HIIT (alternating weeks) |
| Wed | Rest / mobility |
| Thu | Strength B: squat 5×5 · OHP 5×5 · deadlift 1×5 |
| Fri | Cardio: 35 min zone 2 |
| Sat | Strength A or active recovery (long walk) |
| Sun | Rest |

**Diet — calorie awareness phase**
- Track honestly for 2 weeks (mid-July) using any app — learn portion sizes, then stop tracking
- Target: ~300 kcal/day deficit (NOT crash) → 0.5–1 lb/week sustainable
- Bump protein: aim ~1g per lb of bodyweight
- Cut: refined carbs (white rice/bread/pasta) → swap to brown/whole grain or drop entirely on non-training days
- Cut: liquid calories (sugar drinks, fancy coffee) — single biggest hidden source
- Keep: one "free meal" per week — sustainability matters more than perfection

**Expected by Aug 31:** −10 to −15 lbs total, visibly stronger, clothes fit looser, gym is habit.

---

### Phase 3 — Performance + sustain (Sept 1 onward)

**Goal:** finish the −20 lbs (Sept 30 target) + lock in for life. By now this is identity, not goal.

- Diet on autopilot — no tracking, just rules from Phase 1+2 internalized
- Workouts shift to performance goals (5K time, deadlift PR, push-ups in a row — pick one)
- Active recovery on rest days (walks, yoga)
- Reassess every month

## Daily checklist (weekday) — minimum bar

- [ ] Sleep ≥ 7h
- [ ] Caffeine cutoff before 14:00
- [ ] 2L water
- [ ] Protein at every meal
- [ ] Either: workout (Mon/Tue/Thu/Fri) OR walk 7K+ steps (Wed/weekend)

If 5/5 → great day. If 3/5 → fine, recover tomorrow. **Don't aim for 5/5 every day or you'll quit.**

## Things I will NOT recommend

- Crash diets, cleanses, juice fasts, keto-as-fad, IF-as-religion
- Supplements beyond: creatine 5g/day (cheap, well-studied, helps), vitamin D if blood test shows low, protein powder if you can't hit protein from food
- Cardio-only weight loss (you'll lose muscle + rebound)
- Working out fasted in the morning until Phase 2 (energy will tank during cert prep)
- Pushing through pain — soreness is fine, sharp pain means stop

## Common failure modes (read these every check-in)

1. **Going too hard week 1 → quit week 3.** Phase 1 is intentionally easy. Don't "make up" missed sessions by doubling up. Just resume.
2. **Skipping sleep to do more ai-arch.** This is the #1 risk. Tired learning is wasted learning. Trust the 22:30 cutoff.
3. **All-or-nothing diet.** One bad meal isn't a "ruined day". Next meal, back on track.
4. **Comparing to others / instagram fitness.** Your baseline is sedentary 9 hr desk job + family. Different game.
5. **Stopping when life gets busy.** Drop frequency before quality — even 2×/week beats zero. Don't drop to zero.
